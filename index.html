Questions and answers
How do I get started with the use of the XAINDEX platform?

Only affiliate links can be used to register on our platform. We also recommend that you visit the "Quick Start" page of our website, which provides simple steps on how to use XAINDEX and cryptocurrency.

What are your countries of operation?

At the moment we do not have any restrictions on geographical regions or countries. The most important thing is that you have an understanding of how XAINDEX works and that you want to become a user of the platform.

How can I find out more about the platform and where can I get more information?

The platform website (https://xaindex.ai/) contains the most complete and up-to-date information about XAINDEX. In addition, you can learn a great deal from the active users of the platform by searching for "Xaindex" on the Internet.

How do you ensure the security of the user's funds?

We achieve maximum diversification from intruders by using transit channels to receive and send cryptocurrencies. In addition, we do not use the segregated method for the storage of funds, which means that all assets have to be stored within a single system. This provides flexibility for our operational risk policy.

What is the rationale for the payment of the rewards and the bonuses?

Payments of any rewards and bonuses are made through an additional SUT issuance, which occurs during the process of generating a profit by managing platform liquidity using synthetic intelligence and other XAINDEX technologies.

If I want to offer to collaborate, where can I go to do so?

With effective teams and projects that also see potential in the crypto industry, we consider the possibility of interesting and mutually beneficial cooperation. If you want to offer us, please do not hesitate to contact us at coop@xaindex.ai.

If I am a media representative, who should I contact?

Please contact us at press@xaindex.ai if you are interested in promoting our brand or learning more about our vision for the industry.

Where can I find XAINDEX promotional material?

In the "Materials" section of the user account page (https://xaindex.ai/main/account/materials/), all XAINDEX marketing materials are available.
